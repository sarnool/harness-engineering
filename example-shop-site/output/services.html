Booking details

Know how bookings, walk-ins, and changes work before you arrive.

You can book online, call during business hours, or try a walk-in. Weekends are busiest, so booking ahead is a smart move.

Ways to book

  • Online through the website's Book Now button.
  • By phone during business hours.
  • Walk-ins are welcome when time is available.

Changes and cancellations

  • Please give at least 24 hours notice to cancel or reschedule.
  • Late cancellations may incur a small fee.
  • Time estimates are provided when you book.
