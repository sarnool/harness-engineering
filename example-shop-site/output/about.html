About the salon

Meet the team behind Bella's Beauty Salon.

Bella's Beauty Salon has been part of the neighbourhood for over 10 years, with a team that stays current through regular training and specialist focus areas.

Salon story

Experienced care with a relaxed, professional finish.

Whether clients come in for a quick trim or a full pamper session, the goal stays the same: they should leave feeling great. That approach is backed by professional-grade products, careful service, and staff who genuinely enjoy the work.

What supports the experience

  • Kerastase, Dermalogica, and OPI products used in salon and sold in-store.
  • Recommendations available from your stylist or therapist.
  • Qualified staff who keep up with current trends through ongoing training.

Training and products

Why clients trust the team with colour, skin, and treatment choices.

The salon combines brand-backed products, specialist roles, and a simple service style that stays approachable instead of overly technical.

Product partners

  • Kerastase for hair care and home recommendations.
  • Dermalogica for skin treatments and retail support.
  • OPI for nail finishes and salon-quality polish results.

Service standards

  • Colour consultations recommended for major changes.
  • Sensitive skin requests can be flagged at booking time.
  • Regular training keeps the team current with new trends.
