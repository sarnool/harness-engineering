## FAQs

**Do I need to come in for a consultation before a colour appointment?**
For big colour changes (like going from dark to blonde), yes – we recommend a quick chat first so we can give you an accurate quote and timeline.

**How long will my appointment take?**
It depends on what you're getting done. A basic haircut is about 45 minutes. A full colour with highlights can take 2–3 hours. We'll always give you a time estimate when you book.

**Do you cater to sensitive skin?**
Yes! Just let us know when you book and we'll make sure we use products that are gentle and suitable for you.

**Is there a minimum age for services?**
Kids are welcome for haircuts. For waxing and lash services, clients need to be 16 or older (parental consent required under 18).

**Do you offer EFTPOS and card payments?**
Yes, we accept cash, card, and contactless payments. We don't charge a surcharge for card payments.
